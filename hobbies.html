Things That Inspire Me

Hobbies are a great way to relax and learn new things. They help me to stay creative and balanced. Here are some of my favorite activities that I enjoy in my free time.

  • Coding: I love building new websites and experimenting with different HTML structures. It makes me feel creative and logical at the same time.
  • Cooking: I enjoy cooking new dishes for my family. It teaches me patience and creativity, and I love trying different recipes.
  • Mehndi: I have a keen interest in applying mehndi designs. It is an art form that allows me to express my creativity and it also helps me to improve my attention to detail.
  • Learning New Skills: I am always curious to learn about new technologies like Python and AI, and how they can be used to solve everyday problems.
  • Music: Listening to music helps me to focus while studying and coding. It keeps me motivated and relaxed.

These hobbies not only give me joy but also help me to develop skills that are useful in my academic and professional life.
