Areas I Am Working On

It is important to be honest about our weaknesses because it helps us to grow. I am continuously working to improve the following areas.

  • Perfectionism: Sometimes I spend too much time making things perfect. I am learning to balance quality with efficiency.
  • Public Speaking: I feel nervous while speaking in front of a large group. I am practicing to improve my confidence.
  • Taking Too Many Tasks: I tend to take on multiple tasks at once. Now I am learning to focus on one thing at a time.

I see these weaknesses as opportunities to learn. By working on them, I am becoming a better student and a better team member.
